I have no clue what an incremental game is, why am I here

Welcome! I'm glad you made the choice to play this. First, Let's start with resources. In the top left, you should see 3 pictures and 3 numbers. Wood, Stone & Food. These are the heart of the game. Your goal is to get them as high as possible, with no end. Now, let's go to resources and purchase an axe, a pickaxe, and a spear. You are now getting 1 wood, 1 stone, and 1 food per second, split into 200ms gaps. By the way, prestige is used as a term where you reset almost everything or everything, to gain a multiplier, helping you reach bigger numbers faster.

Prestige

You need a total of 1000 of wood, stone & food to gain a prestige point waiting to be claimed. After that goal if reached, the total of each (wood, stone, food) goes back to 0, and the required amount increases by x1.01.

Relocation

Relocating unlocks new content, Such as currencies or tabs. To relocate the first time, You have to spend 250 Prestige Points. (The first relocation is currently the end of the game, however, you can play on.)
