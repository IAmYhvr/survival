Well, what do I do?

Welcome! Under "Manual", Click each button 10 times. Now, purchase an Axe.You are getting 1 wood per second, split into 200ms gaps. Wait until you have 20 (2.00e+1) of each item and purchase a Pickaxe and a Spear.

Prestige

For each building you purchase, you gain prestige points waiting to be claimed. (Tier 1 = 1, Tier 2 = 5, Tier 3 = 25, so on.) Each prestige point is a 1x multiplier, you start with 1.
